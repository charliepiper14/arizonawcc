The Challenge

Why Most Local Meta Ads Fail

1
Wrong Campaign Objectives: Most agencies run traffic or engagement campaigns that get clicks but never convert. For local services, you need campaigns optimized specifically for calls and lead forms.
2
Broad Geographic Targeting: Reaching "everyone in Arizona" wastes budget on people outside your service radius. Precision ZIP code and city targeting is essential for home services.
3
Generic Creative: Stock photos and template copy do not work for local businesses. Homeowners respond to real photos, local references, and offers that feel authentic to their community.
4
No Lead Qualification: Getting 100 leads means nothing if 80 of them are outside your area or not ready to buy. Smart lead forms filter out tire kickers before they waste your time.

Campaign Strategy

Three Phase Approach

Phase 1

Foundation

Week 1 to 2
  • Ads account audit or setup
  • Meta Pixel installation and CAPI configuration
  • Lead form creation with qualifying questions
  • Call tracking implementation
  • Creative production (3 to 5 ad variants)
  • Audience research and targeting setup
Phase 2

Testing

Week 3 to 4
  • Launch initial campaigns
  • A/B test ad creatives
  • Test audiences and placements
  • Monitor lead quality
  • Optimize for cost per lead
  • Daily performance checks
Phase 3

Scale

Month 2 Onward
  • Scale winning ad sets
  • Pause underperformers
  • Expand to new audiences
  • Introduce new creative
  • Build lookalike audiences
  • Weekly optimization reports
